Only 30 prizes are given out per day. Once all 30 are claimed the wheel locks until midnight when the pool resets. This keeps the giveaway fair and sustainable.

7 real prizes: iPhone 17 Pro Max, MacBook Pro, iPad Pro, Samsung Galaxy Tab S9, HP Laptop, $500 Gift Card, and the $3,000 Cash jackpot. There are also 6 "Try Again" segments on the wheel.

Is this really free? ▼
Yes — spinning is 100% free. You only pay for delivery if required AND confirmed by the page admin. Never upfront.
